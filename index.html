About Me

Hello! I am a third-year Electrical and Computer Engineering student at Carnegie Mellon University, with career aspirations in software engineering and computer security. My journey through engineering led me to a fascination with the "invisible logic" of technology, driving me to understand how modern systems are built from the silicon up.

This ECE foundation drives my approach to full-stack application development, where my understanding of computer systems, memory management, and performance optimization is a distinct advantage. I thrive on applying these skills in fast-paced, collaborative environments like hackathons, and I am now focused on channeling this passion toward building software that is not just functional, but also resilient and secure.
